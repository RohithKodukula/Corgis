About

Origins

  • From Wales, UK
  • 10th century Nordish settlers
  • Two Welsh corgi breeds = Pembroke Welsh, Cardigan Welsh Corgi (less common)

Statistical information

  • The smallest recognized herding dog (cattle)
  • Noted for stubby or non-existent tails, but also long tail variants

  • Ranked #11 in intelligence
  • 12 year life span, death from kidney failure, old age, cancer
  • Fox like shape
  • Achondroplastic, naturally dwarf-like in size
  • Several breeding clubs that recognize the value of the corgi, first one in 1925

Behavioral traits

  • Friendly to Strangers
  • Temperature stability
  • Smart, easy to train
  • Good first dog
